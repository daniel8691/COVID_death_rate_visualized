THE CORONAVIRUS

Infections Around the World

Our team has undertaken the task of analyzing COVID-19 data to see how the virus has spread through various countries and regions. We examine infection, death and testing rates (per one million population) for the various countries and hope to gain some insight.

Our Methodology

Our team developed a detailed roadmap to accomplish this task. We had many ideas and desired outcomes to consider before we began, including which data we would use, what we were hoping to learn, who would this analysis help and much more.

Our first steps took place when we decided which data we would use. Next, we would clean and organize our data to provide the most meaningful insights. Once our data was prepared, we created and loaded our flask application so that we can deliver the data to our page. Afterwards, we developed maps and images to help our end-users visualize the story are trying to tell. Finally, we developed our html pages and put all the pieces together.
